Gift Card: A gift card is a prepaid card containing a specific amount of money, used to make purchases at participating merchants. It's a type of prepaid debit card, often given as a gift or used for promotional purposes. Gift cards can be physical (plastic or paper) or digital (e.g., e-gift cards) and may have expiration dates or fees. They offer flexibility and convenience for the recipient to choose their preferred items or services within the card's value.
